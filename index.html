ABOUT ME


Hello, all! My name is Ricardo and I am a fullstack software engineer and recent graduate of the Full Stack Flex Career Path bootcamp at Thinkful. My love for coding blossomed due to my penchant for solving problems and seeing my visions play out to completion. I cannot wait to see where my interests take me as a programmer. I am currently studying Data Science in the Python Language.

As for myself, I currently live in Tulsa, OK. I was born in Toledo, OH, and I have also spent significant time in Las Vegas, NV and San Antonio and Austin, TX. I am a fiance, a father, and a man looking to make a monumental change. I stay physically active by running on various trails, playing basketball, and working out in the gym. I stimulate my creativity through photography of cityscapes, and broaden my horizons through travel (of the 'road trip' variety). Occasionally I will test my luck at the casino, as I am also fond of blackjack and poker.
